# UK/EU Investor Funnel Blueprint (No-Code, $2,000 Budget)

## 1) Positioning

**Offer:** "We help UK/EU investors source high-potential Dubai commercial opportunities with licensed local execution."

**Your role:** AI-powered acquisition + qualification partner.

**Broker role:** Licensed advice, negotiation, and closing.

---

## 2) ICP (Ideal Customer Profile)

Prioritize one core segment first:

- UK/EU-based property investors (age 30–60)
- Portfolio owners or high-income professionals
- Likely ticket size: $250k–$2M
- Interested in yield, diversification, and tax-efficient markets

---

## 3) 90-Day Plan

### Phase A (Days 1–14): Infrastructure

- Set up CRM (GoHighLevel or HubSpot Free)
- Create pipeline: New → Contacted → Replied → Qualified → Booked → Closed/Lost
- Connect tools via Make.com
- Create compliance-safe AI assistant identity/disclaimer

### Phase B (Days 15–45): Outbound Engine

- Build lead list in Apollo + LinkedIn Sales Navigator workflow
- Launch 3-touch LinkedIn sequence + 4-email sequence
- Route replies to AI qualification flow
- Auto-tag lead temperature (Hot/Warm/Cold)

### Phase C (Days 46–75): Conversion Optimization

- Add no-show recovery sequence
- Add nurture sequence (14/30/60-day)
- Add retargeting audience from page visitors and email clickers
- Weekly script optimization using response data

### Phase D (Days 76–90): Scale Readiness

- Document SOPs for VA/support
- Add second country cluster (e.g., Germany or France)
- Introduce multilingual message variants (human-reviewed)

---

## 5) Automation Architecture

1. Lead source (Apollo/LinkedIn)
2. Personalized outreach (AI-assisted)
3. Reply ingestion (email/LinkedIn webhook)
4. AI qualifier (budget, timeline, property type, intent)
5. CRM stage update + score
6. Booking link delivery (Calendly)
7. Calendar confirmation + reminders
8. Advisor briefing note auto-generated before call

---

## 6) Qualification Logic

### Required questions

- Investment budget range?
- Timeline to invest?
- Preferred asset type (office/retail/warehouse/mixed)?
- Cash purchase or financing?
- Primary objective (yield/capital growth/diversification)?

### Lead scoring

- **Hot:** budget confirmed + timeline ≤ 60 days
- **Warm:** budget plausible + timeline 3–6 months
- **Cold:** unclear budget and/or > 6 months

---

## 7) Message Frameworks

### LinkedIn DM #1

"Hi {{FirstName}}, noticed your background in {{Industry}} and interest in alternative property markets. We help UK/EU investors access Dubai commercial opportunities through a licensed local team. Open to a short overview?"

### Follow-up #1

"Quick one, {{FirstName}} — are you currently exploring yield-focused property diversification outside the UK/EU? If yes, I can send a concise market snapshot."

### Email opener

Subject: Dubai commercial opportunities for {{Country}} investors

"Hi {{FirstName}}, we support international investors evaluating Dubai commercial assets with local licensed execution. If relevant, I can share current deal profiles by ticket size and target yield."

---

## 8) Compliance Guardrails

- Clearly disclose assistant is AI-enabled
- Do not claim to be a licensed broker
- No final investment advice without licensed human review
- Final terms/negotiation routed to brokerage professionals
- Respect outreach laws (GDPR/PECR/CAN-SPAM equivalents)

---

## 9) Weekly KPI Dashboard

Track every week:

- New leads added

- Reply rate
- Positive reply rate
- Qualified lead rate
- Booked call rate
- Show-up rate
- Close rate
- Cost per booked call
